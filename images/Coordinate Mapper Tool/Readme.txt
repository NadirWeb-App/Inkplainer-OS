Use this tool to replace these default images with your custom hands/pens. 
1. Simply upload the image, click to mark the tip of the pen
2. Choose the App Export Scale amount, usually 60% is the sweet spot for most hands (this controls the size of the hand in the app)
3. Copy the Full Config, go to the index.html, search for: 
CUSTOM IMAGE-BASED HANDS CONFIGURATION 
and replace the default configs with the new ones
