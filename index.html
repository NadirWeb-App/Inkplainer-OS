Welcome to Inkplainer
The free whiteboard animation maker — no sign-up, no watermark, no cost. Turn images and text into hand-drawn explainer videos in minutes.
1
Add your content
Drop an image onto the right panel, or switch to Text to add a title or label. Each item becomes its own layer.
2
Pick an animation style
Use the Animation tab for fast, ready-to-use styles. Use Drawing for deep control over strokes, outlines, and fills.
3
Generate, then Export
Generate previews your animation live. When you're happy with the result, Export saves it as a video file.

Your content never leaves your device.
